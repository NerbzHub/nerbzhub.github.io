About

Backyard Snowdown was created from a 6 week group project to simulate an indie game dev environment. It included GDD, TDD, scrums, Presentations in front of peers and industry professionals.
After manic brainstorming, we decided on “A snowball fight through the eyes of a kid.” And to round it off I came up with the name Backyard Snowdown. I’ve found that a good name in the planning phase creates excitement to create the game because you almost owe it to the title to create a game.
Once the game had finished beta and entered gold, we created a trailer for the game.
