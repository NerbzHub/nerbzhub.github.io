About

The Six Million Dollar Vigilante Reloaded came about from the third game jam we did. This was chosen as one of the Top Picked games from the jam by the judges. Each team's theme was chosen by a random game name generator. We decided as a team to create a game similar to the arcade classic, Time Crisis. Except instead of guns it's a mouse and you shoot cardboard cutouts of 1950s style gangsters. The idea behind it is that you have 6 bullets and each one is worth a million dollars. This meant that rather than reloading, you use a magnet and pull the used bullets back in to your gun, maintaining your six million dollar net worth.

In regards to the code I worked on in this game, I did everything other than the cutouts going down when hit, magnet reload and the room transitions.

Feel free to poke around the repo with the link attached to the GitHub logo next to the Title.
