About

Bomberang was created from a 2 and a half day game jam with students from AIE. I was one of two programmers that worked on this project. The aim of the game is to not be the one holding the bomb once the timer hits 0. The only thing is, it only explodes when someone is holding it. There is also an area of effect damage with the explosion so if both of you explode, it's a draw. This was chosen as one of the Top Picked games from the jam by the judges.

In relation to my contribution to this project, I implemented the timer, score, audio, collision and lighting.
